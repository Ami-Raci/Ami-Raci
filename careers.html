WHY WORK FOR US?

It's a Wonderful Opportunity

If you like to put a smile on people's faces and brighten up their day, working at Sweetberry would be the perfect opportunity for you! Here, you can learn and develop a variety of skills, such as cooking, customer service, and management, regardless of your experience since we offer training. As for benefits, employees get to eat at our cafe for free, and they can receive bonuses and promotions in addition to their typical salary based on their behavior. Best of all, you can acquire all of these benefits while working at a time that works well for you.


We Care About You

At Sweetberry, team members receive a multitude of benefits because we value our workers. Here, workers choose their work hours in advance. This flexiblity allows them to ensure that their schedule works for their lifestyle. Another way we prioritize our workers' wellbeing is through the health benefits we provide them with, such as access to health insurance and programs that can help our team and their family as well as paid time off. Our paid leave includes sick, maternal and paternal, and vacation leave (the amount of time given is dependent on the time a worker has been employeed and their position). Please contact us if you're interested in learning more about our benefits.
