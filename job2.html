Customer Service Associate

  • Location: 9432 Main Street, Fairfax, VA 22031
  • Schedule: Part-time

Overview

The customer service associate's responsibilities are related to customer service. Their main duties include responding to customer concerns regarding the cafe and taking in-person and online orders. They should be friendly and have execellent communication skills as they are who most customers will interact with, and thus represent the company.

Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Handles customers' orders.

  • Listens to the concerns and feedback of customers.

  • Works with other staff to address customer concerns.

  • Communicates with customers in-person and over calls.

  • Takes orders, calculates their costs, and processes payments.

  • Ensures that customer satisfactory levels stay high.

  • Reports to management when needed.

Skills:

  • Patience

  • Communication

  • Critical thinking

  • Time management

Qualifications:

  • Must be 16 years old or older

  • 1-2 years of customer service is preferred

* Training will be provided.
