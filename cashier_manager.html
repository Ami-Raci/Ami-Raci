Cashier Manager

  • Location: 9432 Main Street, Fairfax, VA 22031
  • Schedule: Full-time

Overview

The cashier manager supervising all cashiers and ensures that they are performing effectively. Their main duties include hiring and training cashiers, resolving conflicts, and overseeing customer service issues. Furthermore, they must be prepared to have staff look to them for leadership, advice, and support.

Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Trains, supervises, and motivates employees.

  • Coaches, counsels, and disciplines employees.

  • Ensures that there is a sufficent number of cashiers at each shift through effective scheduling.

  • Assigns staff tasks that will help to achieve company goals.

  • Regularly reports to management.

  • Proficiently operates cash registers.

  • Ensures that cash is correctly accounted at the end of every shift and properly handles money.

  • Address and resolves issues between both employees and customers appropriately.

  • Ensures a safe, secure, and legal work environment.

Skills:

  • Teamwork

  • Communication

  • Supervising

  • Problem solving & decision-making

Qualifications:

  • Must be 18 years old or older

  • High school diploma, GED, or equivalent is required

  • 2 years of customer service work is required

  • 1-3 years of management is preferred
